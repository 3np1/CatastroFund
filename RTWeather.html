Use this tool to help you understand the current and future weather patterns in your area. Although not all areas are included, most densely populated cities are included such as New York City or Los Angeles.

    Explanation

  • Hover your mouse over the Locations bar below and then scroll down to find the closest location to you. Click your location for more information in a new tab.
  • Remember, some areas are more likely to recieve large storms and some areas only recieve specific types. New York might be more susceptible to blizzards and snowstorms while places like Miami might experience more hurricanes.
